CentOS镜像使用帮助

仓库

  • CentOS7

使用说明

首先备份/etc/yum.repos.d/CentOS-Base.repo

mv /etc/yum.repos.d/CentOS-Base.repo /etc/yum.repos.d/CentOS-Base.repo.backup

方式一

下载对应版本repo文件, 放入/etc/yum.repos.d/(操作前请做好相应备份)

  • CentOS7

运行以下命令生成缓存

curl -o /etc/yum.repos.d/CentOS7-Base-lan.repo http://mirrors.test.com/help/CentOS7-Base-lan.repo
yum clean all
yum makecache

方式二

复制repo内容, 粘贴到/etc/yum.repos.d/repo文件(操作前请做好相应备份)

[base]
name=CentOS-Base
baseurl=http://mirrors.test.com/yum/7/repo/base/
enabled=1
gpgcheck=0
repo_gpgcheck=0

[updates]
name=CentOS-Updates
baseurl=http://mirrors.test.com/yum/7/repo/updates/
enabled=1
gpgcheck=0
repo_gpgcheck=0

[extras]
name=CentOS-Extras
baseurl=http://mirrors.test.com/yum/7/repo/extras/
enabled=1
gpgcheck=0
repo_gpgcheck=0

[epel]
name=CentOS-Epel
baseurl=http://mirrors.test.com/yum/7/repo/epel/
enabled=1
gpgcheck=0
repo_gpgcheck=0

[docker-ce]
name=docker-ce
baseurl=http://mirrors.test.com/yum/7/repo/docker-ce-stable/
enabled=1
gpgcheck=0
repo_gpgcheck=0

[kubernetes]
name=kubernetes
baseurl=http://mirrors.test.com/yum/7/repo/kubernetes/
enabled=1
gpgcheck=0
repo_gpgcheck=0
